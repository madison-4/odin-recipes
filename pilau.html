mapishi ya pilau

Description

pilau is a sweet eat african dish best prepared with beef and rice

There are many types of pilau but this recipe focuses on the one using beef, fried beef

This recipe lays out the steps to prepare beef pilau with rice and beef and several ingredients

I hope you follow these steps and make yourself some good pilau

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Dhania
  • Green Capsicum
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. Assemble the ingredients.
  2. boil the meat pieces till they are dry
  3. Cut the onions and cook them in oil till they become golden brown
  4. Cut the capsicum into small pieces and add them
  5. Pour in the tomatoes and cook them till they become soft or pasty
  6. Add the cooked dry tomatoe pieces and let them fry
  7. Add water two and a quatre time the volume of your rice and let it boil
  8. Add the rice and stir the mixtire
  9. Let the water dry up from the mixture and turn off the heat
  10. Serve the pilau, it's ready
